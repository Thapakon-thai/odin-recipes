Chicken Rice

Description

A flavorful and comforting dish made with tender poached chicken and fragrant rice, commonly known as Hainanese Chicken Rice.

Ingredients

    For the chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3–4 lbs)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 4 slices ginger
  • 4 stalks green onions

    For the rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 2 tablespoons chicken fat or vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 slices ginger
  • 2 ½ cups chicken broth (from poaching)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

    For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    • Rub the chicken all over with salt to exfoliate the skin, then rinse.
    • In a large pot, add chicken, ginger, and green onions.
    • Cover with water and bring to a boil. Reduce to a gentle simmer.
    • Simmer for about 40 minutes or until fully cooked.
    • Transfer the chicken to an ice bath to stop the cooking and keep the skin smooth.
  2. Cook the Rice:
    • Wash the jasmine rice until the water runs clear.
    • In a pot, heat chicken fat or oil, then sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
    • Add rice and stir to coat each grain.
    • Add chicken broth and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover.
    • Cook for 15 minutes, then let rest for another 10 minutes off heat.
  3. Make the Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, chicken broth, and sesame oil.
    • Adjust to taste if needed.
  4. Serve:
    • Chop the chicken into serving pieces.
    • Plate with a mound of rice, sliced cucumbers, and a drizzle of sauce.
    • Garnish with fresh cilantro or green onions, and serve with chili sauce on the side if desired.
