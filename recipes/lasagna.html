Lasagna

Description

A simple and delicious lasagna recipe — perfect for beginners and great for a hearty meal!

Ingredients

    For the meat sauce:

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (24 oz / 700 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste (optional, for richness)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    For the cheese mixture:

  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    Other:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    • Add diced onion and cook for 2–3 minutes.
    • Add garlic and ground beef. Cook until beef is browned.
    • Drain excess fat if needed.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and Parmesan until smooth.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles (skip if using oven-ready):
    • Boil the noodles in salted water according to package instructions.
    • Drain and lay flat on a towel to prevent sticking.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Layer 3 noodles on top.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and sprinkle mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers twice more (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella).
    • Top with extra mozzarella and a sprinkle of Parmesan if desired.
  5. Bake:
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Uncover and bake another 15–20 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
    • Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh herbs.
