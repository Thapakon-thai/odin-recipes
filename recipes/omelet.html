Omelet

Description

a simple and classic omelet recipe

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • 2–3 tablespoons shredded cheese (optional)
  • Optional fillings: diced tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, spinach, ham, etc.

Steps

  1. Crack and Beat the Eggs:
    • Crack the eggs into a bowl.
    • Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
    • Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk until fully mixed.
  2. Heat the Pan:
    • Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the butter or oil and let it melt and coat the pan.
  3. Cook the Eggs:
    • Pour the eggs into the pan.
    • Tilt the pan to spread the eggs evenly.
    • As the eggs begin to set, gently push the edges toward the center with a spatula, letting uncooked egg flow to the edges.
  4. Add Fillings:
    • When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly moist on top, add cheese and any other fillings on one half of the omelet.
  5. Fold and Finish:
    • Carefully fold the omelet in half over the fillings.
    • Cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute until the cheese melts and the inside is fully cooked.
  6. Serve:
    • Slide the omelet onto a plate.
    • Garnish with herbs (like chives or parsley) if desired.
    • Serve immediately.
